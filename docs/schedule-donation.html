How It Works

When you schedule a donation, our team prepares for pickup and ensures your food reaches those who need it most.

📝 Step 1: Schedule

Fill out this form with details about your donation and preferred pickup time.

📧 Step 2: Confirmation

Receive a confirmation email with all the details of your scheduled donation.

🚚 Step 3: Pickup

Our volunteers will arrive at the scheduled time to collect your food donation.

❤️ Step 4: Impact

Your donation will be distributed to those in need, and you'll receive an impact update.
